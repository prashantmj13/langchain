Acme Corp Employee Handbook

Time Off Policy
Full-time employees accrue 15 days of paid time off (PTO) per year, accrued monthly.
PTO requests must be submitted at least 5 business days in advance through the HR
portal. Unused PTO up to 5 days may be carried over into the next calendar year;
anything beyond that is forfeited on December 31st.

Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote arrangements require
manager approval and are reviewed quarterly. All remote employees must be reachable
during core hours, 10am-3pm in their local time zone.

Expense Reimbursement
Business expenses must be submitted within 30 days of purchase via the expense system,
with an itemized receipt attached. Reimbursements are processed within 2 pay cycles.
Meals during business travel are reimbursed up to $75/day.

Parental Leave
Acme Corp offers 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave for the primary caregiver and
6 weeks for the secondary caregiver, available to all full-time employees regardless
of tenure.

Equipment Policy
New employees receive a laptop and a one-time $500 home office stipend within their
first 30 days. Equipment replacements are handled by IT and typically ship within
one week of a request being filed.
