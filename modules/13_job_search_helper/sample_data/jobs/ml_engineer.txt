Title: Machine Learning Engineer

Join our AI platform team building retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems on
top of large language models. You'll design embedding pipelines, evaluate vector
stores, and ship LLM-powered features end to end.

Requirements:
- 2+ years working with LLMs (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI, or similar)
- Hands-on experience with LangChain, LlamaIndex, or a comparable framework
- Strong Python skills, comfortable with embeddings and vector databases (FAISS,
  Pinecone, Chroma)
- Understanding of prompt engineering and evaluation methodology

Nice to have:
- Experience with LangGraph or building agentic systems
- Familiarity with LangSmith or similar tracing/observability tools
